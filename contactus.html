About Reco Bladers

Reco Bladers is a digital ecosystem driven by creativity, technology, and innovation. Founded on November 26, 2025, by Rudra Pratap Sharma and Swastik Prem Srivastava, it has grown rapidly under the visionary leadership of Swastik, who became the permanent CEO on March 8, 2025.
